Here's what you need to know, AJ:

**The good news: your personal account stays intact.**

When you upgrade to the Team plan, it creates a new Claude *organization* — your individual free or paid account (Pro or Max) remains completely separate. You can toggle between them by clicking your initials or name in the lower left, then selecting which account to access.

**How to do it:**

Simply sign into your existing account and visit **claude.ai/upgrade**, then follow the steps to create your Team.

**Important caveat on data:**

Team plan organizations are separate from individual Claude accounts — data cannot be transferred between the two instances. So your existing personal conversations and projects won't carry over to the Team org, but they'll still be accessible when you switch back to your personal account.

**For 5 people, Team plan is the right fit:**

Team plans support up to 150 seats, so you're well within range. You cannot migrate your individual account *into* the Team org — your teammates will need to be added as new members to the organization.

**If you eventually want Enterprise:**

When upgrading from Team to Enterprise, Anthropic recommends keeping the same Team organization and following the upgrade path — that way your memberships, roles, conversations, and projects are preserved.

**TL;DR steps:**
1. Go to **claude.ai/upgrade** while logged into your personal account
2. Choose Team plan and set up your org
3. Invite your 4 teammates to join the org
4. Your personal account remains accessible — just toggle between them
