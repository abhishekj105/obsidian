## 🌡️ Temperature & Electricity Savings from Thermal Cool Roof Coatings in India

### What the Brands Claim

**Dr. Fixit (Pidilite)**
- **Heatshield (Model 602):** Reduces heat conductivity from exterior to interior and lowers inside temperature by up to 5°C.
- **LEC System (spray PUF + polyurethane):** Heat ingress into the structure drastically comes down, reducing energy needs and lowering operational electricity costs every year. It also allows for downsizing AC requirements during initial design.

**Asian Paints SmartCare**
- **Damp Proof:** High sheen brilliant white coating reflects heat and reduces surface temperature by up to 10°C.
- **Damp Proof Ultra:** Provides surface temperature reduction of up to 12°C, with a 12-year waterproofing warranty.

### What Independent Research Shows for India

This is where the numbers get more grounded:

- **Hyderabad field study (peer-reviewed, LBNL/OSTI):** Annual energy savings of 13–14 kWh/m² of roof area from applying white coating to uncoated concrete roofs, corresponding to cooling energy savings of 10–19%.

- **Broader India studies:** Studies in Hyderabad demonstrated that cool roofs could reduce AC electricity consumption by 10–19% for uninsulated concrete roofs, corresponding to 13–14 kWh/m² of annual energy savings — translating to a reduction of about 11–12 kg of CO₂ emissions per square meter annually.

- **New Delhi simulation:** Around 23 kWh/m²-year of electricity can be saved with application of a high-albedo roof with 0.8 solar reflectivity.

- **General industry consensus:** On average, homeowners can expect a reduction in cooling costs ranging from 10% to 30%.

- **Cool Roof Rating Council (global benchmark):** Average energy savings for a cool roof range between 7% to 15% of total cooling costs.

### Honest Summary: What You Can Realistically Expect

| Scenario | Temperature Drop | AC Electricity Savings |
|---|---|---|
| Simple cool coat (Dr. Fixit Heatshield, Asian Paints SmartCare) | 5–12°C on roof surface | **10–20% on AC bill** |
| Advanced PUF insulation system (Dr. Fixit LEC) | 12–20°C on roof surface | **20–30%+ on AC bill** |
| Homes without AC | Improved comfort, indoor drop of 1–4°C | Not applicable |

**As a % of total home electricity bill**, the savings depend heavily on how much AC contributes. In a typical Mumbai/Pune home where AC accounts for 40–60% of summer electricity, a 15–20% reduction in AC usage translates to roughly **6–12% savings on the total electricity bill.**

---

### Key Variables That Affect Your Savings

1. **Floor type** — Top-floor/terrace-exposed homes benefit most; lower floors gain less
2. **Current roof color** — Going from dark/black to white gives the biggest delta
3. **Climate zone** — Pune/Mumbai's moderate humidity means less dramatic savings than Hyderabad or Ahmedabad
4. **AC usage hours** — Higher AC usage = higher absolute savings
5. **Product quality** — Premium systems (PUF + polyurethane like Dr. Fixit LEC) significantly outperform basic elastomeric coats

**Bottom line:** For a top-floor flat in Pune or Mumbai with moderate AC usage, you can realistically expect **10–20% reduction in your AC electricity costs**, which is meaningful but not transformative on its own. These products work best as part of a broader energy efficiency strategy.
